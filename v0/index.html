The Oral History Project

Along with the Museum Initiative, our Oral History Project may be one of our most ambitious undertakings. Collecting and recording interesting encounters and stories from those that met or knew Joe are rapidly being lost to the passage of time as Joe was born over 100 years ago. Our hope is to capture as many audio and visual recordings as possible, but searching out and identifying those with a story to tell presents many logistical and economic challenges. Initially, we plan to leverage a similar project currently underway by the Martinez Historical Society which has a volunteer crew dedicated to capturing stories of early Martinez.
